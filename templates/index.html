    School Bus Services

    Safe, Reliable, and Convenient Transportation for Our Students

    Our school is committed to providing a safe and comfortable bus service to ensure students arrive at school and return home efficiently. Our bus fleet covers multiple routes across the city, making it easier for families to choose a convenient pick-up and drop-off point.



    1. Key Features of Our Bus Service

    Safe and Secure:

    All buses are equipped with GPS tracking, CCTV cameras, and first-aid kits to ensure student safety.

    Each bus has a trained driver and a responsible attendant to supervise the students during their journey.


    Comfortable Rides:

    Our buses are well-maintained, spacious, and fully air-conditioned to provide a comfortable travel experience.

    Comfortable seating and ample space for school bags and belongings.


    Reliable Timings:

    Punctual pick-up and drop-off schedules are maintained to ensure students reach school on time and are safely returned home.

    Real-time updates on bus location and delays are provided to parents through our school's transport app.


    Wide Coverage:

    Our bus routes cover various neighborhoods across the city, ensuring maximum convenience for families.

    Multiple pick-up and drop-off points are strategically planned to minimize travel time



    2. Bus Routes and Timings

    Route Information:

    Detailed route maps are available to help parents find the nearest pick-up and drop-off points.

    Each route is managed by a dedicated bus number and has a clearly defined timetable.


    Morning Timings:

    Buses begin their routes at 8:00 AM, with all pick-ups completed by 9:00 AM.

    All buses arrive at school by 9:00 AM.


    Afternoon Timings:

    Buses depart from school at 2:00 PM and complete drop-offs by 3:00 PM.

    Special arrangements for students participating in after-school activities are available upon request.


    Check Your Route:

    Use our online bus route finder tool [link to tool] to locate the nearest stop and find exact timings for your route.



    Safety and Guidelines for Students

    Student Safety Rules:

    Always remain seated and wear seat belts while the bus is moving.

    Follow the instructions of the bus attendant and driver at all times.

    Avoid standing near the doors and windows.

    Keep the bus clean and respect fellow passengers.


    Emergency Protocols:

    Each bus is equipped with an emergency contact system, allowing drivers and attendants to communicate with the school and parents if needed.

    In case of emergencies, parents will be immediately notified through the school app or SMS alerts.
